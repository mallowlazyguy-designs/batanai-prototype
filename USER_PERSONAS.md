# User Personas for Batanai

## 1. Zimbabwean Sellers
### Description:
Zimbabwean sellers are local entrepreneurs wanting to reach a broader audience. They often have unique products that reflect local culture and craftsmanship.

### Pain Points:
- **Lack of Market Access:** Limited channels to reach international buyers.
- **Payment Processing Issues:** Difficulty in accepting payments from abroad.
- **Logistics Challenges:** Expensive and complicated shipping options.

### Motivations:
- **Increased Sales:** Desire to grow their business and reach international customers.
- **Cultural Representation:** Passionate about showcasing Zimbabwean culture through their products.

## 2. Diaspora Buyers
### Description:
Diaspora buyers are Zimbabweans living abroad who want to purchase local goods either for personal use or to send gifts back home.

### Pain Points:
- **Trust Issues:** Concerns about the quality and authenticity of products.
- **High Shipping Costs:** Often faced with expensive shipping options from Zimbabwe.
- **Limited Product Availability:** Difficulty finding specific products available in Zimbabwe.

### Motivations:
- **Connection with Home:** Desire to maintain ties with their roots and culture through local products.
- **Gift Giving:** Wanting to send gifts to family and friends back home.

## 3. International Customers
### Description:
International customers are global buyers interested in unique goods from Zimbabwe, including crafts, textiles, and food products.

### Pain Points:
- **Shipping Reliability:** Concerns over the reliability of shipping options to their countries.
- **Regulatory Barriers:** Uncertainty regarding customs regulations and import duties.
- **Cultural Understanding:** Limited information on the cultural significance of products.

### Motivations:
- **Unique Products:** Desire to own or gift one-of-a-kind items from a different culture.
- **Ethical Sourcing:** Interest in supporting sustainable and ethical businesses in developing regions.
